About Linux Times

Hello! And welcome to the Linux Times! Here you will find things all about the open source side of computing. This website is dedicated to explaining the use of today's open source operating systems, as well as showing how to begin using open source software. The open source software world is rapidly growing, but most casual computer users don't even know that Linux exists. They believe that Windows is their only choice when choosing a computing platform. That is quite far from the truth. So go check out my posts and start learning about open source software. You don't have to shell out money for an anti-virus or be forced to upgrade to a new version of Windows. In the open source world it really is all about what you want out of your computer. Linux does not hide anything from you, nor are you going to be bombarded with ads on your desktop. If that sounds appealing to you stay a while and keep an eye out for new posts and you will learn about Linux and how to begin using Linux yourself.
